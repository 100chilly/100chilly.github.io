Projects I've worked on

Binary Gaming
Binary Gaming (2011-2015)

This was a gaming community I ran from 2011-2015. I severely underestimated the potential outcome for the community and therfore it was handed over to the australians.

BTC
BinaryTech Coalition

BinaryTech Coalition was a Player-Made Faction for the indie game, Space Engineers. This was a branch of Binary Gaming and is no longer active.

Qubed Q3
Qubed Q3 (2015-2017)

Ambitious project I was associated with. Qubed Q3 was a very diverse gaming community. We specialized in delivering quality fun and entertainment via livestreams and self-hosted game servers. We ended shutting down on October 31st, 2017 due to disagreements.

IVGAS
International Video Game and Anime Syndicate (2017-2018)

A grand project I was associated with. International Video Game and Anime Syndicate (IVGAS) was a new community that didn't just focus on mainstream gaming or even gaming as a whole. We where a very diverse group of people who come from all walks of life and enjoy gaming or anime or even both! We shut down due to over hype killing off our potential gain very early on.

CL
Closet Lolis

A project I was with as Event Team Lead. Closet Lolis is a community created by a few good friends of mine that dedicate their time first and foremost, to lolis. Its a group dedicated to fictional anime characters that are in no way to be lewded/sexualized. The server followed Discord ToS and Guidelines as well as the law. I stepped down as Event Team Lead due to health concerns. The server isn't accepting new people and is decomissioned.

MVGD
Mitsue's Video Gaming Den

Not really a project, but a general place to gather, chit chat and play some video games. If you wanna drop by, please do @ MVGD's Discord
